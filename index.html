About Us

We are a research lab focused on cultural neuroscience, queer theory, and feminist perspectives in cognitive and emotional processes. Our work challenges the traditionally Western-centered view of psychology and neuroscience, focusing on how cultural, social, and gender identities influence cognition, empathy, and memory across diverse populations.

Our Research

Our research spans multiple areas, including empathy, metacognition, minority stress, emotional regulation, and the neural mechanisms underpinning these processes. We explore how societal norms related to gender, sexuality, and culture shape these cognitive processes, especially in marginalized populations.
